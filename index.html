What makes LumTale different?

What is the Lumling style?

Lumling is our own take on a warm, stylized portrait look: soft shapes, gentle colors, and a collectible-toy feel. We don't copy any existing artist or brand — Lumling is a new category we created for LumTale.

Is Door 1 really private?

Yes. Door 1 runs entirely in your browser using local models. Your photo is not uploaded to our servers or any third party. You can verify this by opening your browser's DevTools and watching the Network tab: zero outbound image traffic.

What happens if I use Door 2?

Door 2 sends your photo to a GPU partner to generate a higher-quality Lumling portrait. We do not store or train on your images, and we link to the partner's data-use policy before you upload. We believe you deserve to know exactly where your photo goes.
