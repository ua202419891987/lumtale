What big avatar apps do to non-white faces

It isn't an accident, and it isn't rare. The patterns are well documented:

  • Whitening & warping. Asian, Black, and Latino faces get lightened, narrowed, and reshaped toward a Eurocentric template.
  • Low hit rate. Even popular apps only land ~40% usable results — you pay for 50 images and keep 3–5.
  • Subscription traps. Hidden weekly charges that are hard to cancel once your card is on file.

Honest FAQ

Will my face be whitened or changed?

No. That's the entire point of LumTale. Our generation is tuned to keep your features faithfully yours.

Do you train on my photo?

No. Your photo is used only to generate your portrait and is not stored or used for model training.

Is Door 1 a generator?

No. Door 1 is this promise page. The actual portraits are created in Door 2 (Lumling Studio).

What if I don't want to upload at all?

Then Door 1 is enough — you've learned how we work, with zero upload. You only send a photo when you decide to create a portrait in Door 2.
